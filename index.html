About Membrionex

Membrionex develops solvent-free, high-performance ion exchange membranes for green hydrogen and energy storage systems. Our technology eliminates toxic solvents, ensuring safer and scalable production while supporting low-cost catalysts.

Our mission is to accelerate the adoption of clean energy by enabling cost-effective and reliable electrochemical systems, such as AEM electrolysers and redox flow batteries.

Originating from the College of Design and Engineering at the National University of Singapore, Membrionex combines material innovation with climate impact.

Our Products

Anion Exchange Membrane

Our solvent-free AEM offers exceptional chemical stability and performance in harsh conditions. It is designed for selective ion removal and long-term operation in electrolysers for hydrogen production, redox flow batteries, water purification, electrodialysis, and chemical processing. The membrane supports low-cost, alkaline chemistries and minimizes crossover for higher efficiency.
